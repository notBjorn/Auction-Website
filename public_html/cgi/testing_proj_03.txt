A. Sell an Item
----------------------------
1) Log in as seller@example.com.
2) Click on "Sell an item".
3) Fill the form:
4) Submit.

Expected output: Redirect to the Transactions page which display all items seller has for sale.

B. Bid on an Item
------------------------------
1) Log in as bidder@example.com.
2) Click on "Bid on an item".
3) The pull-down shows auctions currently running (not owned by bidder).
4) Select a running auction and enter a bid HIGHER than the current price.
5) Submit.

Expect output:
   • Success message "Bid Placed" will display
   • New highest bid visible is now visible in the drop down

C. Increase Bid Amount
------------------------------
1) Log in as bidder@example.com.
2) Go to transactions page
3) Scroll down to section 3, current bids
4) Input an increased bid amount and click the button "Raise"

Expected output: Page will reload and the new highest bid will be updated
